About me

For some time, I’ve been learning front-end development through several SheCodes workshops. I started with the basics and gradually moved on to more advanced topics like API integration and artificial intelligence. So far, I’ve completed: SheCodes Basics SheCodes Basics Add-on SheCodes Plus SheCodes Plus Add-on SheCodes Plus AI These courses have given me practical experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, APIs, GitHub, hosting, and even some AI tools. The AI part in particular opened my eyes to how fast technology is evolving, and why it’s important to keep learning. I’m proud to be featured in the SheCodes Hall of Fame, and I enjoy taking part in coding challenges to keep building on what I’ve learned.
